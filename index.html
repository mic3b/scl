Pizza Margherita

This classic pizza is topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. It's simple, delicious, and perfect for a cozy night in with friends or family.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz. fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450Â°F.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border around the edges.
  4. Top with the sliced mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Sushi Bowl

This sushi bowl is a deconstructed version of your favorite sushi rolls, served in a bowl with all of the delicious ingredients layered on top of rice. It's a quick and easy way to enjoy the flavors of sushi without all of the fuss.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked sushi rice
  • 4 oz. sushi-grade salmon, cubed
  • 4 oz. sushi-grade tuna, cubed
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup sliced avocado
  • 1/4 cup pickled ginger
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp. sesame oil
  • 1 tsp. honey
  • 1 tsp. grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tsp. wasabi paste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, honey, grated ginger, and wasabi paste.
  2. Divide the cooked rice between two bowls. Top with the salmon, tuna, cucumber, and avocado. Drizzle with the soy sauce mixture and sprinkle with pickled ginger.
  3. Serve immediately.

Burger

A classic burger recipe that is juicy, flavorful, and easy to make. This burger is made with a beef patty seasoned with salt and pepper, cooked to perfection on the grill or stovetop, and topped with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pickles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill or stovetop to medium-high heat.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine ground beef, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine.
  3. Divide the meat mixture into four equal parts and shape into patties.
  4. Grill or cook patties on stovetop, flipping once, until cooked to your desired doneness.
  5. Place lettuce, tomato, onion, and cheese (if using) on the bottom half of the hamburger bun. Place the cooked patty on top.
  6. Add ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, and pickles (if using) to taste.
  7. Top with the other half of the hamburger bun and serve.
